Features

The first Mynd version will have the essential features. The technical team will keep working on new features and user stories, for every newer version.

Browse by trends or by the newest content

As a user you can browse the pictures added by others. You can choose to view either what's trending or what are the most recent posts. You have the freedom to choose the trends or the newest pictures according to a certain topic or regardless of it.

Find your interests

Unlike the majority of the other photo-sharing platforms, on Mynd you can find what you're interested in not by hashtags but by topics. As a user you can view the images according to the topics they're tagged in. You can also follow the topics that you're interested in.

User interactions

As a user you can add images, tag them according to certain topics, write a description. You can also view other people's photos, comment them, like them and share them on other platforms.
